How to Apply for an IPO?

Step 1

Login to your 5paisa account and select the issue in the current IPO section.

Step 2

Enter the number of lots and price at which you wish to apply for.

Step 3

Enter your UPI ID and click on submit. With this, your bid will be placed with the exchange.

Step 4

You will receive a mandate notification to block funds in your UPI app.

Step 5

Approve the mandate request on your UPI and funds will be blocked.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an IPO?

An Initial Public Offering (IPO) is the process by which a private company becomes publicly traded by offering its shares to the public for the first time.

Can I make multiple applications in same IPO using same UPI on 5paisa?

No, you can only make one application per UPI ID per IPO. However, you can apply through different UPI IDs if you have multiple accounts.

What is cut off price?

Cut-off price is the price at which you are willing to accept any price within the price band. It's typically used for retail investors.

What is book building?

Book building is a process used in IPOs for efficient price discovery. Investors bid for shares within a price band, and the final price is determined based on demand.

What are the acceptable UPI Apps?

You can use any UPI app like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, or any other UPI-enabled app to apply for IPOs.

What are the different investor types in IPO?

There are three main investor categories: Retail Individual Investors (RII), Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIB), and Non-Institutional Investors (NII).
